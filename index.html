About Jaimie Garber

Southern California native Jaimie Garber, certified by the Council for Professional Dog Trainers, passionately provides comprehensive pet care, specializing in strengthening the bond between pets and owners. She cherishes her roles as a professional, wife, and mother.

Born and raised in Southern California, I've dedicated my life to serving our local animal community. My journey in animal care began in 2004 at a local veterinary office, where I gained invaluable hands-on experience.

Pursuing further education, I enrolled in Moorpark College's prestigious Exotic Animal Training and Management (E.A.T.M.) program. This rigorous curriculum provided comprehensive understanding of animal behavior and training techniques across various species. I proudly earned Associate in Science degrees in both Animal Training and Animal Behavior, equipping me with a strong theoretical foundation to complement my practical experience.

To further enhance my expertise, particularly in canine behavior, I undertook the challenging process of certification with the Certification Council for Professional Dog Trainers (CCPDT). This internationally recognized certification demands extensive knowledge and hands-on experience, ensuring that certified trainers meet the highest standards in the field. Achieving this certification underscores my commitment to providing top-tier, scientifically-based training services to our community's dogs and their owners.

My greatest satisfaction comes from transforming relationships between owners and their pets. Watching these bonds grow into lifelong friendships is truly rewarding.

Outside of work, I'm happily married to my supportive husband, Justin Garber, and we're proud parents of two wonderful children. Balancing my roles as a professional, wife, and mother is challenging but immensely fulfilling. Every day, I look forward to making a difference in the lives of animals and their owners while cherishing moments with my family. This commitment to both my community and loved ones forms the heart of who I am and what I do.

In-Home Dog Training

I offer personalized in-home dog training designed to fit your dog's unique needs and your lifestyle. I come to you, making training convenient and comfortable for both you and your furry friend in the Southern California area.

Why Choose In-Home Training?

  • Convenience: No need to travel to a training facility.
  • Personalized Attention: Focused training tailored to your dog.
  • Reduced Stress: Training in a familiar environment.
  • Address Specific Issues: Target problem behaviors where they happen.

  • Basic obedience
  • Leash training

My Qualifications

Being a Dog Trainer Certified by the CCPDT (CPDT-KA) is an esteemed certification and a testament to a trainer's comprehensive understanding of canine behavior and proven training techniques. With such a professional, you are ensuring your dog receives a high-quality, scientifically-informed, and humane approach to training.

Teaching Zoo at Moorpark College (E.A.T.M. Program)

Equipped with hands-on animal behavior knowledge and experience from this prestigious program, graduates bring a unique, multifaceted approach to dog training. This broad educational background ensures a thorough understanding of your dog's needs and the best methods to address them, drawing from experience with diverse species.

Veterinary Experience

A trainer with extensive veterinary experience has a profound understanding of your pet's health, behavior, and overall wellbeing.
