About Jaimie Garber

Born and raised in Southern California, I have dedicated my life to serving our local animal community. My journey began in 2004 at a local veterinary office, gaining invaluable medical and behavioral insight.

I am proud to offer professional dog training to San Dimas, La Verne, and Claremont. I hold Associate in Science degrees in Animal Training and Behavior from Moorpark College's prestigious E.A.T.M. program, ensuring a deep understanding of canine psychology.

Outside of my professional life, I am a proud wife to Justin Garber and mother of two. My commitment to both my community and my loved ones forms the heart of my approach: I treat your dog like a member of the family.
